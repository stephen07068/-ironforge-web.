Since our founding, IronForge has been dedicated to one singular mission: supplying the highest grade structural steel to developers, builders, and contractors across the nation.

Our Story

THE BENCHMARK FOR STRUCTURAL INTEGRITY.

At IronForge Nigeria, we believe that every great structure begins with an unwavering foundation. What started as an ambitious local distribution network has rapidly evolved into a leading national supplier of premium Thermo-Mechanically Treated (TMT) rebars and structural steel.

Our rigorous quality assurance means that every rod we dispatch exceeds both local and international engineering standards (BS 4449 and ASTM A615). We utilize state-of-the-art ultrasonic testing to guarantee flawless internal structures, superior ductility, and optimal carbon-equivalent levels for seamless weldability.

Whether reinforcing an expanding residential estate in Lagos, or laying the groundworks for commercial infrastructure in Abuja, IronForge delivers not just steel, but the peace of mind that comes with certified, uncompromising strength.
